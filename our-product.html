Computing Teaching Made Easy with Group3

Group3's SuperPres simplifies computing teaching by giving your students the power to create, without having to worry about complex setup and arcane Graphics libraries - and without having to use Domain Specific or Antiquated languages.

Simply Launch Group3 SuperPres, click on Start New, and start creating visual, interactive, and engaging lessons, demos and projects.

The perfect next step from Scratch

SuperPres is designed to be the perfect stepping stone in-between Visual Programming such as Scratch and fully-fledged GUI tool-kits such as QT, and a perfect teaching aid for UK GCSE and A-Level courses.
We find that block-coding programs work well for teaching basic logic but neglects actual coding skill and more complex logic, whereas most tutorials for C++ or Java skip the basics and can leave less experienced programmers staring at a blank screen, or leave them with just enough knowledge to be dangerous - but not to be useful.

Levelling Up Your Teaching

Our Platform allows your students to easily take their learning to the next level; without having to overcome the barrier of unfamilliar text interfaces, you can teach your students in more depth and leave them with practical transferable skills - after all, who likes making FizzBuzz anyway?

Projects? Yes Please!

SuperPres contains full Python and JavaScript engines, with all the base functionality you'd expect anywhere else - such as Networking, Threading, and pre-supplied constructs like Quicksort and Queues.
Combined with our multimedia interface, this makes Group3 SuperPres eminently suitable for your students to create interesting GCSE or A-Level projects.

Sshh! No talking in the Library!

If the builtin functionality of Group3 Superpres is not enough for your projects, simply import libraries from the Java Ecosystem and use them within your applications as you would with built-in functions.
